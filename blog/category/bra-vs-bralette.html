Quick Comparison: Bra vs Bralette

Traditional Bras

  • Maximum support and lift
  • Structured cups with underwire
  • Best for larger cup sizes
  • Professional and formal wear
  • Precise sizing required

Bralettes

  • Comfort and natural shape
  • Soft, wireless construction
  • Great for smaller to medium busts
  • Casual and loungewear
  • More forgiving fit
