Common Bra Fitting Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

Common Bra Fitting Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

Introduction

A well-fitting bra can transform your silhouette, provide essential support, and boost your confidence. Conversely, a poorly fitting bra can lead to discomfort, pain, and even health issues. Unfortunately, many women wear the wrong bra size without even realizing it. This guide will walk you through the most common bra fitting mistakes and provide actionable tips on how to avoid them, ensuring you find a bra that feels as good as it looks.

Common Bra Fitting Mistakes

1. The Band Rides Up Your Back

This is one of the most common signs of a poor fit. If your bra band rides up, it means it's too loose and isn't providing adequate support. The band should be firm and level all the way around your torso.

2. Cups Gape or Wrinkle

If there's a gap between your breast and the bra cup, or if the fabric wrinkles, the cup size is too large. This can also happen if the cup shape isn't right for your breast shape.
